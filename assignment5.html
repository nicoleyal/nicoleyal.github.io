Getting Started

This assignment was challenging due to the constraints of the material and the box requirement. I started by looking at box designs and corner joints. I was inspired by a few things that were too complicated for the scope of this project, so I measured the FR1 and started sketching out the basic size and shape.


I experimented with several ways of joining the sides.


After drawing up the plan I realized I should make a cardboard prototype to test my measurements so I found a piece of cardboard that happened to be about as thick as the FR1 and started mapping out my shapes. Then I cut them out with a utility knife.


When I put my cardboard protoype box together I realized I had subtracted the width of the material on the top and bottom when I actually needed to add it. Phew!

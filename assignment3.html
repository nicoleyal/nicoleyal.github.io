Assignment 3: 3D printing + lasercutting lamp!

Step 1: Design Inspiration

After dreaming about awesome lamp designs, I quickly realized that with only one week to do this and the constraints of combining laser and 3D, I would have to scale back to something much less ambitious.

Picking out what inspired me, I really liked the stars and the curvy shape. The reflection made me think of paper lanterns. I decided to switch from hanging lamp to lantern.

Assignment 1: Laser Cut Press Fit Construction Kit!

Getting Started

The process began by absorbing a lot of new information and steps. It was very helpful to go to the Mill and watch classmates with more experience (yay Hannah!) using the laser cutter. I got to witness some troubleshooting.
I measured my cardboard and began brainstorming for the design.

Friday Night at the Mill

I headed back to the Mill on Friday night and found a very peaceful setting to work on my project. The staff was super friendly and helpful.
I got out my calipers and measured my cardboard again to make the correct sized notches.

Troubleshooting

After following the steps in the slides VERY closely, I hooked up to the laser cutter and hit the final print button and nothing happened. A staff member came over and helped me discover that I had to hit the left corner placement in the Illustrator print dialog box. It was a simple fix and then my project showed up on the printer!
